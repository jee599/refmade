Our Philosophy

We don't write a menu and then source ingredients. We visit the farm first — and let the harvest decide.

Every week, our team drives 40 minutes north to three family farms in Gyeonggi Province. What comes back in the truck becomes dinner. The result is a menu that changes constantly — sometimes daily — and a kitchen that stays honest about what "seasonal" actually means.

Glimpses

The Chef

Jiyeon Park

After six years in Copenhagen kitchens — two of them at a two-Michelin-star Nordic restaurant — Jiyeon returned to Seoul with a question: what happens when you apply Nordic discipline to Korean ingredients?

Terre & Table is her answer. The menu is small — never more than 12 dishes — and it changes whenever the farms tell her something new has come in. "The best meal I can serve," she says, "is one I couldn't have planned last week."

"I cook what the soil gives me. If that means three courses instead of seven, then three courses it is."

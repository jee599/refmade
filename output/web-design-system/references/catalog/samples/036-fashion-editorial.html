About

Clothing that
doesn't announce
itself.

Founded in Copenhagen in 2018, Maison exists at the intersection of architecture and wardrobe. Each piece begins as a shape study — how fabric falls, how seams create shadow, how a collar frames the jaw. We design for the person who gets dressed without performing.

All production happens within 400 km of our studio. We work with three mills in Portugal and two ateliers in northern Italy. Every garment ships in recycled packaging with full supply chain transparency.

Values

Made slowly,
worn for years.

We produce two collections per year instead of four. Each piece is tested for 300 wash cycles before it enters production. We'd rather make fewer things that last than more things that don't.

Full supply chain data is printed on every garment tag. Scan the QR code and you'll see where the cotton was grown, where the fabric was woven, who sewed the seams, and how far it traveled to reach you.

Materials

100% organic or recycled fibers. Zero virgin polyester since 2022.

Production

Three mills in Portugal, two ateliers in Italy. All within 400km of Copenhagen.

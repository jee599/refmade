The Maison

Craft distilled to its essence.

Founded by master distiller Elena Kors in a converted greenhouse outside Oslo, Maison Noir began as a single experiment: what happens when you freeze fermentation at its most volatile moment?

Twelve years and four hundred iterations later, the answer lives in every bottle we release. Frost on emerald glass. A spirit that remembers winter.

Philosophy

Atelier

From our greenhouse to your glass.

Process

Freeze distillation at -22°C

Each batch is frozen at the precise moment of maximum volatile expression, then slowly thawed to extract only the purest concentrate.

Material

Hand-blown emerald glass

Every bottle is individually shaped by artisans in Drammen. The emerald tint is achieved through iron oxide, the same mineral found in Nordic glacier water.

Source

Foraged Nordic botanicals

Juniper from Lofoten, cloudberry from Troms, birch sap tapped during the three-week window of Arctic spring.

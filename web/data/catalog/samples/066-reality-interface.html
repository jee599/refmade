About

We don't design screens.
We design comprehension.

Prism Labs is a 9-person spatial design studio working at the edge of what interfaces can be. We partner with founders, product teams, and research labs who believe how something looks is inseparable from whether it works.

Each project in our stack gets one color. One tone. One persistent accent that lives through every component, every state, every handoff. Consistency is the craft.

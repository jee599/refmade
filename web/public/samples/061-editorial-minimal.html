Features

Less interface, more writing

Every detail in Margin is designed to disappear, so your words take center stage.

Distraction-free editor

A clean canvas that hides everything until you need it. Toolbar appears on selection only.

Structured notes

Organize with nested pages, tags, and backlinks. Your knowledge builds on itself.

Daily journal

Automatic daily entries with templates. Build a writing habit without thinking about structure.

Markdown native

Write in Markdown with live preview. Export to PDF, HTML, or plain text anytime.

End-to-end encrypted

Your notes belong to you. Zero-knowledge encryption means we can never read your writing.

Offline first

Works without internet. Sync happens silently in the background when you reconnect.

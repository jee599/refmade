About us

We believe in the power of presence

Founded in 2018, Meridian is an independent studio of 24 strategists, designers, and technologists. We partner with forward-thinking brands to create digital experiences that demand attention.

Our work spans VR installations, interactive websites, AR campaigns, and spatial computing. Every project starts with a question: how do we make people feel something?

Features

Everything you need
for scheduling

From individual bookings to enterprise-grade scheduling infrastructure, BookFlow has you covered.

Smart Availability

Set your schedule once. BookFlow syncs with Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCal to prevent double bookings. Buffer times, minimum notice, and date ranges keep your calendar exactly how you want it.

Team Scheduling

Round-robin, collective, and managed events for your entire team.

API & Webhooks

Full REST API and webhooks to embed scheduling directly in your product.

Video Conferencing

Auto-generate Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams links for every booking.

Smart Reminders

Automated email and SMS reminders reduce no-shows by up to 80%.

Analytics

Track booking rates, popular times, and conversion metrics in real time.
